# MeetScribe AI Roadmap 🗺️

Building the ultimate meeting intelligence assistant.

## 🟢 Phase 1: MVP (Complete)
- [x] Secure JWT-based Authentication.
- [x] Whisper V3 Audio Transcription.
- [x] GPT-4o Summarization and Action Item Detection.
- [x] Dark-mode-first dashboard with glassmorphic UI.
- [x] Local History and Note Management.

## 🟡 Phase 2: Collaboration & Intelligence
- [ ] **Real-time Transcription**: Live WebSocket-based meeting capture.
- [ ] **Speaker Diarization**: Automatically identifying who said what.
- [ ] **Custom Templates**: Summary styles optimized for Sales, Engineering, or Board meetings.
- [ ] **Calendar Integration**: Connect with Google/Outlook to auto-sync meeting schedules.

## 🔴 Phase 3: Enterprise & Automation
- [ ] **AI-Driven Follow-ups**: Automatically send action items via Email or Slack.
- [ ] **Team Workspaces**: Collaborate on meeting notes with multi-user editing.
- [ ] **Enterprise SSO**: SAML and OAuth for organizational security.
- [ ] **Mobile App**: Native iOS and Android apps for on-the-go capture.
